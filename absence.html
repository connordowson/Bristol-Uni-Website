Absence Reporting

Information:

In the first instance for any unplanned absence please complete one of the following methods:

  • Ring the Undergraduate Office at Gloucestershire Academy on 08454 22 6233.
  • Email the Undergraduate Office at Gloucestershire Academy on Undergraduate.Department@glos.nhs.uk.
  • Complete the form to the right (before 9.30am on the day). We will notify your Unit Coordinator/Unit Tutor in Gloucestershire.

Please state:

  • Your Name
  • Year
  • Phone Number
  • Reason for absence and length of time likely to be off
