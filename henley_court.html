Accomodation

Henley Court

A 16 bedroom property situated on Montpellier Place, Gloucester accommodating 4th year students. The property is split into 1 & 2 bed flats all with bathroom and/or ensuite, kitchen/diner and lounge area. There is NO parking at this property.

All of the properties in Gloucester are newly furnished with beds, a wardrobe, a drawer unit, desk & chair and with fridge & freezer, microwave, toaster, kettle, ironing boards (but NOT irons), vacuum cleaners (see note below), dining tables, chairs and soft seating.

PLEASE NOTE : For all of the properties in Gloucester you will need to bring basic cooking utensils, crockery, cutlery, pots & pans (although a limited supply may be available), duvet, pillows, bed linen and towels as they are not provided.
