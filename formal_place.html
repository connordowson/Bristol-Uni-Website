Formal Place

A 23 bedroom property situated on Southgate Street, Gloucester accommodating our 5th year students. The property is split into a mix of 1 & 2 bed flats all with bathroom and/or ensuite, kitchen/diner and lounge area. There is NO parking at this property although there is an NCP car park at the rear of the accommodation.

All of the above properties in Gloucester are newly furnished with Bed, Wardrobe, Drawer Unit, Desk & Chair and with Fridge & Freezer, Microwave, Toaster, Kettle, Ironing Boards (but NOT irons), vacuum cleaners (see note below), dining tables, chairs and soft seating. PLEASE NOTE : For all of above properties in Gloucester you will need to bring basic cooking utensils, crockery, cutlery, pots & pans (although a limited supply may be available), duvet, pillows, bed linen and towels as they are not provided.
