Leaving Details

Request for leave in advance:

You can request to leave though the use of the form below or alternatively if you would rather you can directly email the department.

Requests for leave should be submitted in advance to Academy Medical Dean (for Academy based teaching) via Undergraduate Office at Gloucestershire Academy. Email: Undergraduate.Department@glos.nhs.uk giving information below:-

  • Name and Student Number
  • Year of Study
  • Reason for leave
  • Date of leave (from-to), no of days
  • Detail of teaching missed
  • How will you make up this teaching time
  • How many days leave (including illness) already taken in acacdemic year
  • Attach any evidence or note any evidence that can be supplied

Notes for Students:

  • A request for leave will not be granted for more than two weeks in any Unit as an absolute maximum.
  • Requests for leave on health grounds over 5 days must be accompanied by a medical certificate.
  • You should minimise time off particularly during clinical Units.

Clinical Students:

Absences during the clinical years are likely to have a more serious impact on your learning than absences during pre clinical teaching. There may also be particular weeks/days where it is your only opportunity to receive teaching/experience in particular specialties. If your absence is not on health grounds or for a critical personal problem you should take this into consideration.

Please note that if you take a long period of leave during a Unit, and then also have to take time off for illness, you may fail the Unit due to non attendance.

Requests for leave during external SSC Units: SSC Co-ordinator – via Year 3 or 5 Year Co-ordinator at Bristol.

Requests for leave during any other teaching: Programme Director via relevant Year Co-ordinator at Bristol.

Please note leave of 4 days and over will need to be countersigned by the Programme Director Student Affairs, please allow time for this to happen.

The Programme Directors (Student Affairs) will follow-up on the welfare of any student who has had substantial time off. If you are refused a leave of absence you may ‘appeal’ to the Academy Dean via your Year Co-ordinator at the Centre for Medical Education.

Evidence may be requested particularly for longer leave requests or if you have already had long periods of absence.
